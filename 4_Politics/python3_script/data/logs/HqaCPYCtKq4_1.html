Published on Jun 7, 2013

Former JFK assassination suspect and Irving resident Buell Frazier and acclaimed journalist Hugh Aynesworth share their memories of November 22, 1963 during this program presented by the Irving Heritage Society.
Frazier gave Lee Harvey Oswald a ride to work on the day of the assassination. Aynesworth wrote "JFK: Breaking the News," and witnessed some of the events of that day.
