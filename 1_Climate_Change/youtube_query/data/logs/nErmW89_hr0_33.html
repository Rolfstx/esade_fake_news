Having a baby in prison is no picnic. Every year thousands of babies are born in American prisons. Many of them are taken away from their mothers at birth or soon after. These powerful, moving, shocking and insightful films follow the dramatic and emotional stories of a group of pregnant inmates at the Indiana Womenâ€™s Prison.
